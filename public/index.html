GEGIS Student Resource Hub

The GEGIS Student Resource Hub is a dedicated platform for students at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology pursuing Geomatic Engineering and Geospatial Information Systems (GEGIS) and Geospatial Information Systems(GIS). It provides centralized access to curated academic materials, including lecture notes, past papers, practical resources, and reference content designed to support learning and academic excellence.

Built to simplify access to essential course content, this platform enhances collaboration, encourages independent study, and ensures students stay up to date with their coursework. Whether preparing for exams, working on projects, or strengthening key concepts, the GEGIS Hub serves as a reliable academic companion throughout your studies.
